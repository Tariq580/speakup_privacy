Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to your iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → SpeakUp → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of the current billing period.

How do I get a refund?

All subscriptions are processed through Apple. To request a refund, visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID. We cannot process refunds directly.

Why isn't my microphone working?

Go to iPhone Settings → SpeakUp → Microphone and make sure it's enabled. The app requires microphone access to record your speech.

How accurate is the AI feedback?

Our AI uses advanced language models (OpenAI Whisper for transcription, Anthropic Claude for analysis) to provide highly accurate feedback. However, AI feedback should complement, not replace, human coaching for professional speaking engagements.

Is my audio data private?

Yes! Your recordings are processed securely and used only to generate feedback. We never sell your data. See our Privacy Policy for full details.

Can I use SpeakUp offline?

No, SpeakUp requires an internet connection to send your recordings to our AI servers for transcription and analysis.

What languages are supported?

Currently, SpeakUp supports English only. We're working on adding more languages in future updates.

How long are recordings stored?

Your practice sessions and transcripts are stored as long as your account is active. You can delete individual sessions anytime from the app. To delete all your data, contact us at the email above.

Does SpeakUp work on iPad?

SpeakUp is designed for iPhone. While it may run on iPad, the experience is optimized for iPhone.

I found a bug. How do I report it?

Email us at tariqarian972@gmail.com with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version. Screenshots help too!

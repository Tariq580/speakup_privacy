Getting Started

How do I start practicing?

1. Download SpeakUp from the App Store
2. Create an account (Apple, Google, or Guest)
3. Choose a practice topic
4. Tap the record button and speak
5. Get instant AI feedback!

Do I need to pay to try SpeakUp?

No! Your first practice session is completely free with no credit card required. Try it out, and if you love it, upgrade to Premium for unlimited practices.

What topics can I practice?

We have 30+ topics including: Job Interviews, Elevator Pitch, Presentations, Weekend Stories, Favorite Hobbies, Travel Memories, and more. New topics added regularly!

Subscription & Billing

How much does Premium cost?

Premium Membership costs $7.99/month and includes unlimited practice sessions with full AI analysis.

How do I cancel my subscription?

Go to iPhone Settings → [Your Name] → Subscriptions → SpeakUp → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

Can I get a refund?

Refunds are handled by Apple. Visit reportaproblem.apple.com and sign in with your Apple ID to request a refund.

Features & Functionality

What does the AI analyze?

Our AI analyzes: Speaking pace (words/minute), Filler words (um, uh, like), Content clarity, Sentence structure, Vocabulary usage, Confidence level, and overall delivery.

What is the "10/10 Version"?

The AI generates a perfect version of your speech showing what it would sound like at a 10/10 level. This helps you learn by example and see exactly what to aim for.

How long can my practice sessions be?

Practice sessions can be up to 5 minutes long. This is the sweet spot for effective practice and detailed feedback.

Can I save my practice history?

Yes! All your practices are automatically saved. Review past sessions, track your progress, and see how much you've improved over time.

Privacy & Data

Is my voice data safe?

Absolutely. Your recordings are encrypted in transit, processed securely by our AI partners (OpenAI and Anthropic), and never sold to third parties. See our Privacy Policy for details.

Technical Support

Why isn't the microphone working?

Check: Settings → SpeakUp → Microphone → Make sure it's enabled. If still not working, try restarting the app.

Do I need an internet connection?

Yes, SpeakUp requires internet to process your recordings through our AI servers.

Which iOS version do I need?

SpeakUp requires iOS 16.0 or later.
